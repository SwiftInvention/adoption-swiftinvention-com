Email list and website

Eastern European Adoption Coalition (EEAC) www.eeadopt.com was a great resource for me especially early in the process. Go to the website, then click on the EEAC Support Groups link, and browse the available elists to find one that applies to your situation. For Russian adoption we used the list called A-PARENT-RUSS.

I've posted several specific questions and got fabulous info and help. I asked for people to respond to me personally rather than post (that prevents you from having to read the daily listings, which can take up to 2 hours a day to read!). You can still get help and advice all the way through parenting issues. I no longer have time....
