International Adoption

  • There were over 20,000 international adoptions to U.S. in 2002. Of those, approximately 5,000 children were from Russia, 5,000 from China, 2,200 from Guatemala and 1,800 from South Korea. (note 2)
  • Almost 90% of internationally adopted children are less than 5 years old. 46% under 1 year old, 43% 1-4 years old. (note 1)
  • International adoptions have more than doubled in the past 11 years. (note 1)
  • Adoptions overall in the US have been running about 120,000 per year (domestic and international combined) for the past decade. (note 2)

(note 1)Source: Evan B. Donaldson Adoption Institute www.adoptioninstitute.org (circa 2002)

(note 2)Source: Adoptions's New Geography, The Wall Street Journal, October 14, 2003
