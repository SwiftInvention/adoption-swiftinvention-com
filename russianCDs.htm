Russian Language CDs

1. Adopting in Russia

Do this course! I spent about 20 min. a day over 2 months and really memorized the full course. It was critical to our communication with the girls in the first 2 months and extremely helpful during travel. You can order a CD/tape and booklet from Teresa Kelleher by sending an email to Adopttlc@aol.com, or by writing to:

TLC Communications
P.O. Box 90
Taylor, AZ 85939-0090

It costs about $45 for the tape version and about $50 for the CD version. She donates 10% of her profits to overseas orphanages. It's adoption specific. Just the right key phrases and words for comfort and basic needs.

2. Pimsleur

If you really get ambitious we highly recommend Pimsleur Language Program Russian I (Comprehensive Programs). The audio program is quite good if you are not interested in learning written characters.
